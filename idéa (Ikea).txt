Game idea:
Castlevania similar tracks to collect materials/blocks later used to build your own base/village on your flying ship that has crashed (in a safe zone where you can farm materials).
You need pieces to repair the ship (some pieces require making with machines).
You will need residents in your village that you acquire by completing miniboss tracks.
You can also use the material you have created to create armour and weapons and build machines and the inhabitants can do certain things for you.
When the ship is repaired, you can go and defeat the big boss.
From the boss, you get a gear that allows you to go to the next world and so on.
(ps to get to the levels you need to go thru the doors that are hidden in the safe zone)
